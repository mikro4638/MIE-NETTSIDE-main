About Me

My name is Mie Elisabeth Kronkvist, I am 23 years old and from Oslo. I moved to Bergen in the summer of 2023 and am now in my final year of the Bachelor's program in Cognitive Science at the University of Bergen. The program combines IT and psychology, focusing on how people think and interact with technology.

Outside of my studies, I am an active and social person who approaches both everyday and work-related challenges with a positive and solution-oriented mindset.
